What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting critical systems and sensitive information from digital attacks. Cybersecurity measures are designed to fight threats against networked systems and applications.

Why is Cybersecurity Important?

Cybersecurity is important because it protects sensitive data from unauthorized access, ensuring data integrity, confidentiality, and availability.

Top Cybersecurity Threats

  • Ransomware: Software that locks files and demands a ransom to release them.
  • Phishing: Fake messages designed to steal sensitive information.
  • Malware: Software designed to disrupt or damage a computer system.

Best Cybersecurity Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Keep your software up-to-date.
  • Be cautious of phishing scams and suspicious emails.
