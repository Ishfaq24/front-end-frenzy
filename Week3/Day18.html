What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks.

Top Cybersecurity Threats

  • Ransomware: A form of malware that encrypts a victim's files, demanding payment to restore access.
  • Phishing: Fraudulent attempts to steal personal information through deceptive emails or websites.
  • Malware: Malicious software designed to damage or disable computers and systems.

Best Cybersecurity Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Keep software and systems up to date.
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links.
